Our Key Metrics

Our innovation: Whilst on the outside they seem like simple gloves, ThermaGloves host intricately designed layers of pure comfort and thermal delight. The outside will be a weatherproof durable layer with grip on the fingers so you can continue to do things such as use your phone. The inner layers consist of a sodium acetate solution encased in a soft malleable plastic. This is what changes based on temperature. The final layer is a soft cotton layer that is a breathable soft fabric for the ultimate comfort. With a comfortable velcro wrist strap they fit hands/wrists of any size. They will come in many different colour combinations so we can satisfy all of our audience.


Our gloves are different from others due to the technologically advanced layers of interchangeable incalescence. This eliminates discomfort in the hands throughout different seasons.
